A Novel Method for Breast Cancer Detection Using KNN

Abstract

Breast Cancer is a leading cause of death among women, so early and accurate detection of the cancer can help to decrease breast cancer mortality rates. In detection of abnormalities, computer-aided detection plays an especially significant role for radiologists. Screening programs helps to detect breast cancer early, thus enabling easier treatment and higher rates of survival as compared to late-stage cancers. This study aims to develop a novel approach for detection of breast cancer using K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN). This report is made by conducting a thorough literature review, employing appropriate research methodology, and prescribed an algorithm to predict the cancer on mammography images. We have achieved an accuracy of 68% using the proposed approach.

Display System for Spatial Content Exploration in Museums

Abstract

This study introduces an interactive display system for research science museums, designed to enhance spatial content exploration. The system utilizes a projector-camera setup to track a white sheet of paper and dynamically project content onto it. This enables a hands-on, engaging experience for visitors, allowing them to interact with digital exhibits intuitively.The proposed system offers a novel approach to displaying complex subjects such as geographic maps human anatomy, and historical artifacts. By combining object tracking and real-time projection the system fosters an immersive educational environment. This research aims to improve the accessibility and effectiveness of museum exhibits by integrating interactive digital elements.

A REVIEW PAPER ON INCREASING EFFICIENCY OF SOLAR CELLS USING ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND NANOTECHNOLOGY

Abstract

With the increasing demand of electricity, the conventional sources of energy is running out. So, scientists have introduced alternating sources of energy to meet the demand of the energy. Solar energy is one of the alternating energy sources. In this energy conversion process, solar cells absorb direct and indirect sunrays and convert it into electrical energy. But main problem with solar cells is its efficiency and storage. The solar cells can reach upto 42% of its efficiency. Nanotechnology on the other hand is the most developed technology which can help increasing the generation and storage of solar energy. Using of nanoparticles in energy sector can help boosting the efficiency and generation of the solar cells by increasing the light scattering. Whereas artificial intelligence (machine learning), helps scaling up the manufacturing of solar cells. This paper presents a detailed review on how to increase the efficiency and generation of solar cells along with the storage using nanoparticles and artificial intelligence.

Design and Analysis of P&O algorithm based MPPT solar system in PSIM

Abstract

Conventional solar panels suffer with non-identical losses (resistive losses, diode leakage, loss of material properties etc.). To overcome these unwanted phenomena, a solar maximum power point tracking (MPPT) algorithm has been introduced. In this paper, the algorithms used for tracking the sun (or the solar maximum power point tracking algorithm) has been introduced & a brief description of the basic & easiest algorithm i.e., the P&O Algorithm using PSIM software has been provided to give a clear view about the concept & implementation of MPPT.
